Docat E-commerce Platform

Docat is an innovative platform designed specifically for e-commerce applications.

  • Tailored for online retail businesses
  • Supports secure transactions and user management
  • Built for scalability and modern user experience

Project Overview

Docat is a specialized e-commerce company dedicated to providing high-quality dog and cat food. The platform offers a wide selection of premium pet nutrition products, making it easy for pet owners to find and purchase the best food for their furry companions. Docat focuses on reliable delivery, secure shopping, and excellent customer service to ensure a seamless experience for pet lovers.

Key Features
  • Real-time product catalog and inventory management
  • Secure payment gateway integration
  • User authentication and profile management
  • Order tracking and notifications
  • Admin dashboard for analytics
Technologies Used
  • Flutter & Dart
  • Firebase Authentication & Firestore
  • Stripe Payment Integration
  • Cloud Functions
